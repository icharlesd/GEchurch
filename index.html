Who We Are

About GEM Church

Glorious Evangelical Ministries (GEM) is a Pentecostal church in Thoothukudi, South India. We believe in God the Father, the Son Jesus Christ, and the Holy Spirit. Our mission is rooted in James 1:27 — pure and undefiled religion: caring for orphans and widows in their distress.

01
Faith
We stand firm in belief in God the Father, Jesus Christ the Son, and the Holy Spirit.
02
Mission
Rooted in James 1:27 — caring for orphans and widows, serving the vulnerable in our community.
03
Evangelism
Proclaiming the gospel of Jesus Christ and making disciples across Thoothukudi and beyond.
04
Community
A warm, welcoming family — walking together in love, care and genuine fellowship.
