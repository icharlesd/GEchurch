Our Story

A Living Heritage
of Faith

Founded in 1892 along the banks of the Periyar River, St. Thomas Mar Thoma Church has been a spiritual home for generations of faithful families in Kochi. Our congregation traces its roots to the ancient Christian traditions of Kerala — a faith planted by the Apostle Thomas himself on Indian soil.

We are a community of believers who celebrate the richness of our Syrian Christian heritage while embracing all who seek the living God. Our liturgy is offered in both Malayalam and English, honouring the beautiful culture of this blessed land.
